Porter's Five Forces Analysis

Electric Two-Wheeler Industry

Industry Rivalry
Impact Level: Intense
  • Fragmented market with many players
  • Rapid market growth
  • Continuous innovation
  • Pricing pressure
Threat of New Entrants
Impact Level: Moderate to High
  • Capital and technology barriers
  • Government support and incentives
  • Brand loyalty and market penetration
Threat of Substitutes
Impact Level: High
  • Traditional ICE vehicles
  • Public transport and ride-sharing
  • Range anxiety and infrastructure
Bargaining Power of Suppliers
Impact Level: Moderate
  • Supplier landscape and availability
  • Switching costs
  • Technological dependency
Bargaining Power of Buyers
Impact Level: High
  • Consumer awareness and information
  • Price sensitivity
  • Low switching costs and brand loyalty
